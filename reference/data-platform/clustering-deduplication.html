ğŸ—“ï¸ Past attemptsÂ¶

  • Consideration of https://github.com/dedupeio/dedupe: but seeing that the comparator evaluates 2 values at a time = O(nÂ²) complexity at runtime, and with our volumes (~500K actors) = concern about going with a non-vectorized approach. We see user feedback in that direction (matching time exploding when going from a 1K to 5K sample).
